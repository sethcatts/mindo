How to do stuff

Creating Tasks

You can start creating tasks in Mindo by clicking on the text area at the top of the page

Tasks can be added by clicking on the "+" button to the right of the text area or by hitting enter on your keyboard

By default your tasks will be added to list 1 which will always be the list closest to the top of the page followed by list 2 and list 3

Selecting a List

Mindo can have up to 3 different lists at any time

Adding a task to one of the lists is as easy as selecting the list you want your task added to and creating the task as normal

While typing a task you can quickly switch between which list you will be adding to by using the up and down arrow keys on your keyboard

Closing and Marking Tasks

Tasks can be marked and unmarked by clicking anywhere on the task itself

Marking a task is a way to indicate that the task is complete but still be able to read it or unmark it should it need to be revisited.

To close a task click the close button on the right side of the task

Additional Information

Tasks are stored locally in your internet browser. This means that even if you restart your browser or computer your task lists will still be here.
